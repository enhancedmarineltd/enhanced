Who we are

Enhanced Marine Limited is a leading international subsea equipment rental and solutions provider for the global offshore energy sector.

Through our three service lines – survey & robotics, mechanical solutions and asset integrity – we provide specialist equipment, advanced technologies and services to support our customers' offshore renewable energy and later-life oil & gas projects.

Survey & robotics

Providing state-of-the-art ROVs, autonomous underwater vehicles, and advanced sensor technologies for precise subsea mapping, inspection, and oceanographic data collection.

Discover more

Mechanical solutions

Delivering high-performance subsea tooling, cutting equipment, and bespoke mechanical engineering solutions.

Discover more

Asset integrity

Ensuring the longevity and safety of critical offshore infrastructure through advanced non-destructive testing (NDT).
