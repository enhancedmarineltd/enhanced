About Enhanced Marine

Who we are

Global subsea expertise, local commitment. Since 1985, we have delivered safe, innovative solutions across the energy lifecycle.

40+ years of excellence
23,000+ assets
15 global locations
Our purpose

Delivering energy solutions, safely and sustainably

Enhanced Marine Limited is a leading international subsea equipment rental and solutions provider for the global offshore energy sector. Through our three service lines – survey & robotics, mechanical solutions and asset integrity – we provide specialist equipment, advanced technologies and services to support our customers' offshore renewable energy and later-life oil & gas projects.

Our team of experts works closely with clients to engineer bespoke solutions that reduce risk, improve efficiency, and extend the life of critical infrastructure.
